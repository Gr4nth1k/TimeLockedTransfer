FAQ
Is it non-custodial?
Yes. Only the recipient can claim. Only the sender can cancel before unlock.
Is the fee refundable?
No. The 1% fee is taken at schedule time and is non-refundable.
Can I cancel after unlock?
No. After unlock, only the recipient can claim the funds.
